Best for Pets

Allow your pet to look and feel their best with our Best for Pets grooming near Phoenix! Regular grooming is an important part of pet ownership. While many dogs and cats do a pretty good job of cleaning themselves, they still need some help from their human companions from time to time. They need to have their nails and coats trimmed to ensure both their comfort and their health. They may also need things like ear cleaning, treatment for fleas or ticks, anal gland expression and other services.

Dog and cat grooming prices depend on your pet's breed, size, coat and temperament. During your pet's grooming session, we'll:

  • Trim their toenails
  • Clean their ears
  • Brush their teeth
  • Cut their hair
  • Express their anal glands
