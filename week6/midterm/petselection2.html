Pet Selection:

All Breed Dog and Cat grooming available. We use Environmentally friendly products. Full Service includes: Nail Trim, Ear Cleaning, Anal Gland Expression, Bath with conditioner as needed. Our Professional Groomers have been in the business for over 15 years, and bring experience from all over the country and Arizona. Breed Pricing is available when you call our Salon at 480-990-8070. We pride ourselves in our professional handling and care we give each customers pets, and work with your family to accomplish the desired result. All pets are groomed in the order of their arrival. More time will be required when bringing multiple pets. All pets arriving with Flea’s or Tick’s will be given a bath to remove them. All daycare grooming will be done as late in the day as possible, and before your scheduled pick up time. Additional charges for RUSH, Flea and Tick, DE matting and other special arrangements will be incurred.
